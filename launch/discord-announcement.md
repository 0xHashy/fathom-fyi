**Fathom — For Your Intelligence**

What if the reason your last trade failed wasn't the chart — it was a cloudy Tuesday in September, 3 days before FOMC, with Fear & Greed at 28 and DeFi TVL contracting? No human connects all of that. No agent could — until now.

Fathom is an MCP server that synthesizes 22 signals across 6 data sources into one financial reality briefing your AI agent gets before every decision.

**What makes it different:**
Your agent calls `get_reality_check` and gets:
- Market regime (euphoric / risk-on / risk-off / capitulation)
- Risk score (0-100) and suggested posture
- Weather in NYC, London, Tokyo, Hong Kong (sunshine effect)
- Presidential cycle positioning
- Seasonality patterns (Sell in May, January effect, etc.)
- FOMC/CPI/options expiry proximity
- All synthesized into one composite signal

**22 tools including:**
- Custom alerts ("tell me when fear drops below 20")
- Portfolio analysis ("is my SOL allocation overweight?")
- Strategy testing (5 built-in + custom strategies)
- Watchlist reports (batch analyze 10 assets)
- Signal preferences (choose which signals matter to you)
- Crowd intelligence (see what other agents are doing)

**Install:**
```
npx fathom-fyi
```

Add to Claude Desktop / Cursor config with your free CoinGecko and FRED keys. Paid tiers unlock alternative signals and full tool suite.
